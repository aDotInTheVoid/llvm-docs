template<typename To, typename From>
struct llvm::CastInfo< To, Optional< From > >

Provide a CastInfo specialized for Optional<From>.

It's assumed that if the input is Optional<From> that the output can be Optional<To>. If that's not the case, specialize CastInfo for your use case.
